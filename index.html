About Achan's Bellam Pootharekulu

Achan's Bellam Pootharekulu also called Jaggery Paper sweet which is indigenous to a village named Atreyapuram. Consequently, they are also called Atreyapuram Bellam Pootharekulu. These jaggery-coated paper sheets are most popular during festivals and celebrations by bringing extra sweetness into your life.

Jaggery Putharekulu provides benefits like an instant energy boost from jaggery's natural sugars, improved digestion and satiety due to fiber, and a dose of essential minerals, vitamins, and antioxidants from the jaggery, dry fruits, and rice flour. It can also help with fatigue and provides nutrients that support overall well-being, though it's important to consume it in moderation as it's still a sweet treat.
